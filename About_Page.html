We all use Internet, but how much do we know about who invented and how it works? Well here it is, browse through this site to know about how internet works!

Internet is made of an incredibly large number of independently operated networks what's interesting about the system is that it's fully distributed there's no central control that's deciding how packets are routed or where pieces are networked or build or even who interconnects with whom these are all business decisions that are made independently by the operators they are all motivated to assure that there is end-to-end connectivity of every part of the network because the utility in the net is that any device can communicate with any other device.
